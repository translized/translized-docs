Translation History will allow you to see the history of the translations for a specific key. You will also see exact day when the translation was made.

:::tip
By beeing able to see the history of the translations, you can easily spot which translation had better impact on your users.
:::

## Revert Translation

Once you opened **Translations History** modal, clicking the "revert button" you can revert a translation to the previous version.

:::info
Translation History modal can be opened by clicking the "Translation History" option under respective language.
:::
