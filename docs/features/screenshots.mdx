You can attach screenshot images to each key. This can be usefull to give better context of how and where will key translation be used inside your projects.

## Upload Key Screenshot

To upload screenshot, simply go to specific key detail page and either drag & drop screenshot image or click on drag zone to select your image.

:::tip
By clicking on uploaded screenshot image, you can zoom it in full size.
:::

## Remove Key Screenshot

To remove the screenshots, hover over the screenshot image and click on the ‘Delete screenshot’ button.
