# Screenshots

You can attach screenshot images to each term. This can be usefull to give better context of how and where will term translation be used inside your projects.

## Upload Term Screenshot

To upload screenshot, simply go to specific term detail page and either drag & drop screenshot image or click on drag zone to select your image.

:::tip
By clicking on uploaded screenshot image, you can zoom it in full size.
:::

## Remove Term Screenshot

To remove screenshot from term, hover the screenshot image and click on "Delete screenshot" button that will popup.
